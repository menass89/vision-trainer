Frequently asked

What is Vision Trainer?

A daily practice for sharpening contrast vision, built on perceptual learning. Each session shows faint striped patterns and asks which flash held the pattern; the difficulty adapts to your level as you go.

How long is a session?

A few minutes. Perceptual training rewards consistency over length, so sessions are short and meant to be done regularly.

Do I need an account?

No. There is no sign-in and no cloud. Everything lives on your device.

What is the dichoptic / red/cyan mode?

An optional mode that separates what each eye sees using red/cyan channels. It is there for people who like to train each eye differently; it requires red/cyan glasses.

Will my data sync to a new phone?

Not currently. Because everything is stored locally for privacy, your history stays on the device it was created on.

Is this for eye health?

Vision Trainer is a perceptual training tool for visual practice. It does not perform professional assessments or provide professional recommendations. If you have any concern about your vision, please see a qualified eye professional.
