Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

The short version: we collect nothing. Vision Trainer has no account, no server, and makes no network calls for its features. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Vision Trainer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There is no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party data SDKs.

What stays on your device

Your training history, contrast-sensitivity results, settings, and streak are stored only in local storage on your device. This data never leaves your phone and is not accessible to us or anyone else. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Notifications

If you turn on the optional daily reminder, the app schedules a local notification on your device. This is handled entirely by iOS on your device. No reminder data is sent anywhere.

Children

Vision Trainer does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected at all, no personal information about children is gathered.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, for example if a feature is added that would change how data is handled, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at visiontrainer@gmail.com.
