How Vision Training Works

Perceptual Learning
Your brain can learn to see better. Neurons in V1 cortex become more sensitive to contrast through repeated practice with Gabor patterns — the same signals your eyes naturally detect.
Lateral Masking
Flanking patterns train your brain to suppress noise and amplify signal. Like tuning a radio — same signal, less static, clearer picture.
Published Evidence
Polat et al. (2004) — 1 line Snellen improvement
Polat et al. (2009) — FDA Phase II: 70% gained ≥1 line
