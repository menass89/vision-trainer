Train the way you see.

A quiet daily practice for sharper contrast vision. Adaptive exercises that meet your eyes exactly where they are, with your progress kept entirely on your device.

How it works. Each short session shows faint striped patterns and asks one thing: which flash held the pattern? An adaptive engine tunes the difficulty trial by trial, just beyond easy. These are the conditions under which the visual brain keeps adapting.

What you get

  • Adaptive sessions that find your threshold automatically.
  • Your contrast sensitivity drawn over time as your own personal curve.
  • An optional dichoptic mode with red/cyan separation.
  • A focused, low-distraction interface built to keep your eyes on the task.

Yours alone

No account, no sign-in, no cloud, no analytics, no tracking. Your training history lives on your device and never leaves it.

A note on what this is

Vision Trainer is a training and practice tool for visual perception. It is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or provide care for any condition, and it is not a substitute for professional eye care. If you have any concern about your vision, please see an eye-care professional.
